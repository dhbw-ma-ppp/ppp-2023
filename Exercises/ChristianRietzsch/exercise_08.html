age_survived = data.groupby('Age')['Survived']

age_survived.sum('Survived').plot(color="blue")
leg = age_survived.count().plot(color="red", title="Number of Survivors by Age")
leg.legend(["Survivors", "All People"])

The red graph shows all people based on their age.
The blue graph shows how many people of a certain age survived.
You can see that most people were between 15 and 35 years old.
Therefore the number of survivors in this age bracket is the highest.
